Item 60: The patient is taking medication with a questionable efficacy and/or unfavourable safety profile e.g.: venotropic or vasodilatory drugs (e.g. cinnarizine, naftidrofuryl, pentoxifylline, piracetam). Questionable efficacy; Increased risk of adverse effects (e.g. orthostatic hypotension and falls) and interactions. 1st Re-evaluate whether indication is still present and prefer stopping therapy; 2nd Prefer non-pharmacological approach, a drug with a favourable risk/benefit ratio for a specified time, or refer to physician.
